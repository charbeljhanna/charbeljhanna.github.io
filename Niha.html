A village in the highland of Batroun District, 40 km away from the City. Its altitude is 1300 m. one can reach it from batroun via Ijdabra, Bejdarfel, Kour valley, Sourat, Rachkedde, Helta, Dael, Kfarhelda and Kfour El Arbi. It has several churches the oldest of which being St-George’s (Mar Gerges, ~360 years) and the “Lady of Nouh’s” church (Saydet Nouh, ~120 years). It also has a Cedar Forest reaching the “Hadath Aj-Hebbeh’s” Forest in Bsharry District.
